🔥 CAULDRON — HOW IT WORKS
WHAT IS THIS?
Cauldron syncs all instructors' phones to one timer. The Chief controls everything — everyone else just watches their screen and runs their belt group.
STEP 1 — CHIEF INSTRUCTOR
1Open the app → tap CHIEF INSTRUCTOR
2A 4-letter code appears (e.g. XKQR) — tell this to all instructors
3Wait for instructors to connect (you'll see a count)
4Tap FIRE IT UP 🔥 — hype intro plays, then timer auto-starts
STEP 2 — EACH INSTRUCTOR
1Open the app → tap your belt color (Yellow, Orange, Green, Blue, or Brown)
2Type the Chief's 4-letter code → tap SYNC UP
3Your screen now mirrors the Chief's timer automatically
CHIEF CONTROLS
▶Play/Pause — pausing shows SIKE screen on all phones
◀▶Step arrows — jump back/forward one phase (60s or 30s block)
⏮⏭Round arrows — jump to previous or next full round
●Round dots — tap any dot to jump directly to that round
60sPhase bar — tap 60s/30s blocks to jump to that phase
↺Reset — goes back to Round 1, Phase 0
MUSIC (CHIEF ONLY)
⏮⏭Previous / next track
⇄Shuffle mode — random next track when song ends
⚙Open music library — select track, add new Suno URLs, delete
📱Music plays through Chief's Bluetooth speaker. Instructors see visual sync only.
IPHONE — IMPORTANT
!Open in Safari (not Chrome)
!Tap Share → Add to Home Screen — required for music to keep playing when screen locks
NO WIFI?
Tap Solo / no wifi on the instructor join screen to run your belt independently without syncing.
THE ROUNDS
Each round = 60s technique overview + 4 × 30s steps. 8 rounds total. Finisher shown at the end.
